About Us

Premier Shipping & Logistics Company

Albo Global Logistics is a premier shipping and logistics company established in 2016, headquartered in the heart of Lagos Island, Nigeria. We specialize in providing comprehensive freight forwarding, cargo handling, and delivery solutions that connect Nigeria with global markets. Our strategic partnerships with industry leaders like FedEx, UPS, and DHL enable us to offer world-class logistics services with local expertise and personalized attention.
